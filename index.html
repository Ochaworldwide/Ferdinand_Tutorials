Welcome To Ferdinand Tutorials

Ferdinand Tutorials is an online Educational platform that helps science and Agro (Fishery) students to easily understand their course of study through Visual and written contents which are made available on the different E-learning platforms.

Our Services

Online Jambites Tutorials

We prepare Science courses like Physics, Chemistry and Biology for our Jambites. We make these subjects more easier and understandable than they appear to students.

Online Fishery Teachings

We prepare Fishery videos to educate and guide you on how best to startup, grow and Manage your fish farm for optimum productivity.

Online Intro Biochemistry Tutorials

We teach and advice young Biochemists on the best way to understand the course and also use the knowledge of Biochemistry to solve Societal Problem.

Online YouTube Career Mentorship

Here we guide you on the best ways to Optimize your YouTube Channel and also Mentor you in all the courses we offer here on this Channel

Our Mision

To be the best Education platform in different African countries through our Educational write-ups and powerful video presentations

Our Vision

To Build an Educational platform, that will solve the Academic challenges of Science and Fishery Students around Africa.
